Orenda and the lost owl

2D Platformer, Unity/Fmod







Orenda and the lost Owl is a 2D platformer, it’s a game in which you play as Orenda, who must repair a totem pole at the top of the mountain.


In this project I did the sound design and participated in the creation of the music.


I integrated the sounds into unity using C# scripts with the development team.
